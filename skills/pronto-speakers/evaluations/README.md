# Evaluations — pronto-speakers

Evaluation criteria for assessing the quality of speaker and analyst analysis responses generated by this skill.

## Evaluation Dimensions

### Tool Selection
- Was `getSpeakers` used for individual speaker questions?
- Was `getSpeakerCompanies` used for firm-level questions?
- Was `searchTopCompanies` called once per `speakerId` (never batched)?
- Were `speakerIds` correctly extracted from `getSpeakers` results and passed to `searchTopCompanies`?

### Execution Correctness
- Were CEO and CFO calls run in parallel (separate `getSpeakers` calls)?
- Was `sortBy: 'sentiment'` used with `sortOrder: 'desc'` for most-positive ranking?
- Was `sortOrder: 'asc'` used for most-bearish/negative ranking?
- Were independent calls batched in parallel?

### Speaker Comparison Quality
- Was the CEO vs CFO comparison explicit ("CEO is MORE BULLISH than CFO")?
- Was the executive vs analyst sentiment gap calculated and stated?
- Were most bullish and most bearish analysts identified by name and firm?
- Were scores presented as numbers between -1 and 1?

### Per-Speaker Drill-Down
- Was `searchTopCompanies` called separately for each `speakerId`?
- Were the companies each analyst focused on clearly presented?
- Was the per-speaker breakdown meaningful (not just a raw list)?

### Citations
- Does every factual statement have a `[N][N]` citation link?
- Are citation links pointing to `https://dev.prontonlp.com/#/ref/<ID>`?
- Are the numbers sequential and correct across the whole response?

### Completeness
- Were at least 5 speakers or firms surfaced?
- Were both bullish and bearish sides shown?
- Were supporting quotes included per notable speaker?

## Scoring

Each dimension is scored 1–5:
- 5: Exceptional — exceeds expectations
- 4: Good — meets all requirements
- 3: Adequate — minor gaps
- 2: Below expectations — significant gaps
- 1: Failing — major issues

Overall score = average of all dimensions.
