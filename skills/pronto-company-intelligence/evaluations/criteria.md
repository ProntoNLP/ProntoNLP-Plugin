# Evaluations

Evaluation criteria for assessing the quality of company intelligence reports generated by this skill.

## Evaluation Dimensions

### Completeness
- Did the report include all sections appropriate for the selected template?
- Were all relevant Pronto tools called?
- Were parallel execution batches optimized correctly?
- Was getAnalytics run per-quarter (not just one aggregate call)?
- Were charts generated with all 10 graph types?
- Were getSpeakers called separately for CEO and CFO (not just all executives)?

### Accuracy
- Do stock performance numbers match the tool outputs?
- Are analyst estimates correctly transcribed?
- Are sentiment scores accurately reported per quarter?
- Are RISING/FALLING labels correct based on the data?
- Are quotes properly attributed?

### Insight Quality
- Does the executive summary explicitly state sentiment direction (RISING/FALLING)?
- Does the executive summary explicitly state investment score direction (RISING/FALLING)?
- Does the executive summary state stock reaction to earnings calls?
- Does the executive summary state forecast tone direction (IMPROVING/DETERIORATING)?
- Are trends from getTrends interpreted meaningfully?
- Is the competitive comparison actionable?
- Are risk factors prioritized by severity?
- Are divergences between sentiment and stock price highlighted?
- Is the CEO vs CFO sentiment comparison present with ABOVE/BELOW labels?
- Is the Executive vs Analyst sentiment gap calculated and interpreted?
- Does the report state whether executives are MORE POSITIVE or MORE NEGATIVE than analysts?
- Are most bullish and most bearish analysts identified by name and firm?

### Tool Usage
- Were independent tools called in parallel?
- Was companyId from getCompanyDescription passed to stock/prediction tools?
- Were competitor companyIds from getCompanyCompetitors passed to getStockChange (per competitor)?
- Were documentIDs from getCompanyDocuments passed to per-quarter getAnalytics?
- Was getTrends called with the companyName parameter?
- Were forecast sentences searched per earnings call using documentIDs?
- Were getSpeakers called for Executives_CEO and Executives_CFO separately?
- Were date ranges kept within the 1-year analytics limit?

### Charts & Visualization
- Were Chart.js charts rendered inline in the response?
- Does it include all 10 charts (sentiment, investment, stock reaction, dual-axis overlay, 1Y price, competitors, trends, events, analysts, exec vs analyst)?
- Are earnings call dates marked on the stock price chart?
- Are positive/negative values color-coded correctly (green/red)?
- Is the competitor chart sorted by performance?

### Presentation
- Are tables formatted correctly in markdown?
- Are quotes properly attributed with speaker, role, and date?
- Is the quarter-over-quarter comparison table present with Direction columns?
- Is the report structured with clear section breaks?

## Scoring

Each dimension is scored 1-5:
- 5: Exceptional — exceeds expectations
- 4: Good — meets all requirements
- 3: Adequate — minor gaps
- 2: Below expectations — significant gaps
- 1: Failing — major issues

Overall score = average of all dimensions.
