# Evaluations — pronto-sector

Evaluation criteria for assessing the quality of sector and topic analysis responses generated by this skill.

## Evaluation Dimensions

### Tool Selection
- Was `searchSectors` used for sector-level distribution questions?
- Was `searchTopCompanies` used for company ranking within a topic?
- Was `getAnalytics` called first to identify event types before `searchTopCompanies`?
- Was `search` used only for quote enrichment (not as a primary tool)?
- Was `getTrends` only used when the word "trends" was explicit in the query?

### Execution Correctness
- Were independent tool calls batched in parallel?
- Was `searchTopCompanies` called once per event type (not batched together)?
- Was the date range within the 1-year analytics limit?
- Were structured params (`eventTypes`, `aspects`, `sectors`) used instead of free-text where applicable?

### Response Quality
- Were at least 5 companies or sectors presented as examples?
- Was a ranked table included (sector/company | sentiment score | mention count)?
- Were supporting quotes included per notable result?
- Was the distribution or ranking interpreted meaningfully (not just listed)?

### Citations
- Does every factual statement have a `[N][N]` citation link?
- Are citation links pointing to `https://dev.prontonlp.com/#/ref/<ID>`?
- Are the numbers sequential and correct across the whole response?
- Are full IDs used including digits after the hyphen?

### Completeness
- Were at least 5 companies or sectors surfaced?
- Was the sector/topic interpretation actionable (not just a data dump)?
- Were both positive and negative sides shown when relevant?

## Scoring

Each dimension is scored 1–5:
- 5: Exceptional — exceeds expectations
- 4: Good — meets all requirements
- 3: Adequate — minor gaps
- 2: Below expectations — significant gaps
- 1: Failing — major issues

Overall score = average of all dimensions.
